<TITLE> THREE SHOT DEAD IN S AFRICAN TOWNSHIP - WITNESS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> THREE SHOT DEAD IN S AFRICAN TOWNSHIP - WITNESS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAFRICA-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JOHANNESBURG (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least three blacks were shot and killed Saturday in a township east of Johannesburg, a witness said. </P>
<P> The witness, Joseph Chatatso, told reporters men from a migrant workers hostel in Tokoza walked into a house nearby and killed the three in their sleep. </P>
<P> The hostel is a stronghold for the Inkatha Freedom Party which has waged a decade-long feud with the African National Congress of Nelson Mandela. </P>
<P> The shooting took place near the scene of a similar attack a week ago in which four people were killed. </P>
<P> Police could not immediately confirm the killing but said there had been shooting in the township. </P>
