<TITLE> THOUSANDS GREET ARAFAT ON THIRD DAY IN GAZA</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> THOUSANDS GREET ARAFAT ON THIRD DAY IN GAZA</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-ARAFAT-GAZA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GAZA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Thousands of Palestinians cheered PLO chief Yasser Arafat's motorcade Sunday as he began his third day of a historic homecoming to the Gaza Strip, witnesses said. </P>
<P> The crowds waved flags and posters as his car, escorted by a dozen military vehicles, traveled the two miles from Palestine Square in Gaza City to a citrus factory, his first stop on a full day of visits. </P>
<P> A member of the Palestinian Authority appointed under the Palestinian Liberation Organization's self-rule deal with Israel said Arafat would also visit a hospital under construction and a college and university in Gaza. </P>
<P> His visit to the self-rule enclave in Jericho is expected either late Sunday or Monday. </P>
